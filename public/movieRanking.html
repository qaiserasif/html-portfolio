The Best Bakeries in Karachi According to Qaiser

My top 3 bakeries according to quality of food

Rehmat-e-Shireen

This is the best bakery in Karachi. They have variety of sweets and their taste is amazing and they have maintained a good quality of foold for a long time

United Kings

They are very famous for their Gulab Jamun. They also make very good and savory pizza

Kababjees Bakers

They are new entrants but gained popularity very quickly due to their restaurant. They provide good quality food but in cheaper price
